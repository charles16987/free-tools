How to Use Image Crop & Resize Tool

This free online image editor allows you to crop, resize and download images instantly. No signup or software installation required.

Step-by-Step Guide:

  1. Click the Upload button and select an image.
  2. Use the 8 big crop handles to adjust the crop area.
  3. Move and zoom the image freely inside the 400×400 editor box.
  4. Click the Apply Resize button to generate the preview.
  5. Click Download JPG to save your edited image.
