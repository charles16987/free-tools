How to Use This PNG to JPG Converter

  1. Click the Choose Images button and select up to 10 PNG files.
  2. Click the Convert to JPG button to generate JPG images.
  3. Preview the images in the gallery.
  4. Click the Download JPG button under each image to download individually.
  5. Or click Download All as ZIP to download all converted images at once.

This PNG to JPG converter is perfect for anyone who wants to reduce image file size or convert files for compatibility. It is useful for:
