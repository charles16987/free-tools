How to Use This Image Compressor

This tool is very simple to use, even if you are not a technical person. Follow these steps:

  1. Click the Choose Images button and select up to 10 photos.
  2. Adjust the quality slider (lower = more compression, higher = better quality).
  3. Click Compress for each image or use Compress All.
  4. Download your compressed images one by one or download everything as a ZIP file.
