Addressing the User Pain Point: Why This Tool?

Standard image filenames (like `IMG_1234.jpg`) are bad for SEO. They don't tell search engines what the image is about. Manually renaming dozens of files is tedious and error-prone.

This tool solves that by:

  • **Automation:** Renaming up to 20 files in one click.
  • **SEO Focus:** Enforcing a **keyword-dash-number** format (e.g., `blue-running-shoes-1.jpg`).

Frequently Asked Questions

1. How does SEO-friendly renaming work?

Search engines prefer descriptive filenames with keywords separated by dashes. We automatically convert your original filename (e.g., `IMG001.jpg`) to your keyword + a sequential number (e.g., `your-keyword-1.jpg`).

2. Are my images safe?

Absolutely. Your images never leave your device. All renaming happens inside your web browser using JavaScript, ensuring **100% privacy and security**.

3. Can I rename more than 20 images?

The current limit is **20 images** per batch to ensure fast, stable, and browser-friendly performance. You can run the tool multiple times for larger batches.
