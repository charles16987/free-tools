What Is a PDF Compressor?

A PDF compressor is a tool used to reduce the file size of a PDF without losing its readability or quality. It is useful for email attachments, online form submissions, and storing large documents. This tool uses smart compression methods to maintain clarity while reducing size.

Why You Need to Compress PDFs

Large PDF files take more time to upload, share, or download. Compressed PDFs load faster and save storage space. This is especially helpful for students, job seekers, office users, and designers who handle large files.
